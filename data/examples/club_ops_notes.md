# Event follow-up workflow notes

The student organization runs one event planning meeting every Tuesday. Each meeting creates around 22 follow-up tasks per week across sponsors, volunteers, room setup, and promotion.

Right now the president and operations lead copy notes out of Google Docs, rewrite the action items into a spreadsheet, send a recap email, and manually check who replied. That rewrite and follow-up work takes about 6.5 hours per week.

Sponsor follow-up can take 48 hours because questions wait for manual approval and then someone has to draft a clean message from rough notes. We also lose time tracking down who owns which task after the meeting.

About 15% of the action items need rework because the first recap misses a due date, owner, or meeting detail. People often wait on manual status updates before moving their work forward.
