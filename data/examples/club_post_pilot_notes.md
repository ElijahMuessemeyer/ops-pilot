# Post-pilot review notes

The pilot ran for 4 weeks on weekly event follow-up. After week two, the team standardized the meeting note template and the operations lead only had to approve edge-case sponsor messages.

Manual follow-up dropped to 3.8 hours per week from the original 6.5. Sponsor follow-up now averages 30 hours instead of 48. Rework fell to about 8% and on-time completion is roughly 96%.

Two blockers surfaced:

- sponsor outreach still needed manual approval for edge cases
- one volunteer report format was inconsistent in week two

Adoption is around 88% now that the note format is stable.
